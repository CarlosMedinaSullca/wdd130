The purpose of having an online presence is to provide open access to our recreation services, visual content to describe the experience and places, and contact information in order to increase our customers

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find the options for rafting like places, dates, prices, security recomendations, content of past experiences.
